BIO PORTFOLIO

Empty Staircase

Photography, 2025

This piece transforms a quiet European street into a surreal dreamscape through dramatic color manipulation and atmospheric lighting. The green and magenta tones shift the familiar architecture into something otherworldly, creating a sense of mystery within an otherwise ordinary setting. The reflective cobblestone ground and the looming cathedral-like structure in the distance add depth, guiding the viewer’s eye upward through the composition.

At the center of the scene, a lone figure stands on the staircase, draped in flowing fabric that contrasts sharply with the rigid geometry of the surrounding buildings. This solitary presence, combined with the eerie palette, evokes a cinematic and slightly unsettling mood—like stepping into a world caught between reality and fantasy. The work invites viewers to imagine the story behind the figure and the strange, enchanting city they inhabit.
