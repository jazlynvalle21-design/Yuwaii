"Popping Boba Brusts Flavor"

Typography Art, 2024

This playful typographic illustration celebrates the bright, explosive energy of popping boba. Each word is designed with its own visual personality—soft, bubbly forms for “boba,” scattered multicolored bursts for “burst,” and dynamic letterforms for “flavor.” The composition mimics the joyful chaos of bubbles popping, using vibrant colors and rhythmic shapes to create a sense of movement and excitement.

By blending expressive hand-drawn lettering with bold digital color, the piece transforms typography into a lively visual experience. It captures the fun, sweetness, and explosive surprise of a familiar treat, turning text into an energetic celebration of flavor.
