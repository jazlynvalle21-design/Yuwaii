Everything Arbotek

Typography art, 2024

This dynamic typographic pattern explores repetition, rhythm, and visual hierarchy through the word “Arbotek.” Combining multiple weights, scales, and orientations of the same type, the composition creates a dense, energetic field of text. The alternating contrasts between bold and thin letterforms guide the eye across the design, while the black-and-white palette emphasizes structure and form over color.

The piece investigates how typography can function as both language and texture. Through controlled repetition and variation, the design transforms a single word into a layered visual environment—bold, architectural, and rhythmically alive.
