BIO PORTFOLIO

GEISHA

Digital Illustration, 2022

This digital illustration explores the elegance and artistry associated with traditional geisha culture. The portrait highlights the iconic white makeup, floral kanzashi ornaments, and richly patterned kimono, each element carefully rendered to reflect ceremonial beauty. Soft shading and stylized details emphasize grace and poise, bringing modern digital techniques into dialogue with historical aesthetics.

Through this piece, the artist celebrates the cultural significance of adornment and performance, capturing a timeless expression of femininity, tradition, and craftsmanship.
