BIO PORTFOLIO

Mystical Deersa

Digital Photography, 2025

This piece explores the enchanting yet unsettling allure of mythical forest creature. The "Mystical Deersa" serve as both guides and temptations, their luminous presence pulling travelers deeper into the woods. Their elegance and soft glow create a sense of calm that masks a more ambiguous intention, blurring the line between safety and danger. By portraying the deer-like creature as both beautiful and otherworldly, the artwork captures the tension between curiosity and caution that the forest evokes.
