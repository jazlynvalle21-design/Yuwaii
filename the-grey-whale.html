The Grey Whale

Digital Illustration, 2023

This digital illustration blends realism and fantasy through the quiet encounter between a massive whale and a solitary lighthouse. Suspended above the water, the whale appears both weightless and monumental, transforming the familiar coastal landscape into a surreal moment of wonder. By merging natural imagery with dreamlike impossibility, the piece explores themes of scale, serenity, and the boundary between the real and the imagined. The work invites viewers to pause and consider the calm tension between land, sea, and sky.
