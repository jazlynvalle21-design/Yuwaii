Fetch Up Couture

Animation, 2024

This commercial animation follows a girl who is overwhelmed while trying to choose an outfit, surrounded by clothes yet feeling like she has “nothing to wear.” Her frustration sets the tone as she searches through piles and drawers without success. The pacing, expressions, and movement emphasize the relatable chaos of getting ready, capturing a moment many viewers instantly recognize.

Just when she’s about to give up, the doorbell rings. A mysterious package arrives, revealing a magical wardrobe filled with unlimited outfits. As the wardrobe bursts open with light and possibilities, the animation shifts into a playful, energetic sequence showcasing the endless transformations. The piece highlights creativity, self-expression, and the joy of discovering new styles, all while presenting the magical wardrobe as the perfect solution to her everyday struggle.
