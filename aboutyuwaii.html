ABOUT YUWAII

Yuwaii is the personal creative brand of artist and designer Jazlyn Valle. It represents my visual identity as a multidisciplinary creator working across illustration, graphic design, photography, and animation. I created Yuwaii as a space to showcase my artistic voice — one defined by playful storytelling, bold color choices, and whimsical, character-driven imagery. The brand allows me to bring my interests together, from children’s book illustration to digital design, and present them as a cohesive creative practice.

Yuwaii reflects my belief that art should be expressive, imaginative, and engaging. Whether I’m designing graphics, developing characters, or experimenting with mixed media, I aim to create work that feels warm, memorable, and visually unique. As my portfolio continues to grow, Yuwaii serves as both a personal identity and a creative foundation for future projects, collaborations, and professional opportunities.

Yuwaii also represents my growth as an artist. It is a place where I document my creative evolution — from early sketches and class projects to refined illustrations and polished design work. Every piece within Yuwaii shows a part of my artistic journey, reflecting the skills I continue to build and the ideas I’m learning to express more confidently. The brand reminds me that creativity is always in motion, and that every project is an opportunity to grow, experiment, and explore new possibilities.

Looking forward, Yuwaii is a foundation for my future goals. As I move deeper into the worlds of illustration and visual storytelling, Yuwaii will become the home for larger personal projects, collaborations, and professional work. Whether itevolves into a studio identity, a children’s book portfolio, or a creative brand of products, Yuwaii is designed to grow with me. It represents not just the work I make today, but the imaginative, meaningful work I hope to create in the years ahead.
