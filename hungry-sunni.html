BIO PORTFOLIO

Hungry Sunni

Animation, 2024

This short video captures a humorous moment with a cat named Sunni, who nibbles his owner’s foot to signal that he’s hungry. The playful interaction highlights the everyday personality of pets and the small, ordinary moments that become unexpectedly memorable. Through this simple exchange, the piece reflects the bond between humans and animals and the way pets communicate their needs in their own expressive ways.

After his belly is filled up, he goes into a food coma.
